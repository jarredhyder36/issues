Kobe "The Black Mamba" Bryant remains the Greatest Laker

When Kobe Bryant was traded to the Lakers in a draft-day swap, nobody thought an athletic guard out of high school could turn into the greatest player in franchise history. Now, 20 years and five titles later, here's why Kobe Bryant is the greatest player to wear purple and gold…

  1. Widely considered the greatest by other Laker greats
  2. Tied for most titles in Laker franchise history
  3. Won two championships with less help than the other greats had
  4. 3rd in All-Time Scoring including his historic 81-point game
  5. 2000-2001 Kobe and Shaq had what is known as one of the best teams in NBA history
  6. Kobe has countless records for the Lakers which will be very hard to break, ever

    7. Kobe has one of the most if not the most legendary legacy with a single franchise. He stayed with the Lakers throughout his whole 20-year long career and brought many wins, championships, buzzer-beaters, as well as keeping the Lakers on top and sustaining the great franchise it has always been. If you don’t appreciate what Kobe Bryant did for the Lakers that says more about you than him.
